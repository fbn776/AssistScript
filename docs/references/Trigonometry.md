## sin
`sine`<br/><br/>
**Description:** <br/>Returns the sine of the given angle in radians.<br/>

**Syntax:**<br/>

`sin <angle>`<br/>


**Example:**<br/>

```asrc
sin 90
```

**Result:**<br/>

```asrc
0.8939966636005579
```
